Music Therapy

Music therapy is an evidence-based approach to improving mental health and well-being. Our music therapists work with individuals to:

  • Manage stress and anxiety
  • Express emotions and feelings
  • Enhance relaxation and mindfulness
  • Promote self-awareness and personal growth

Whether through listening to music, playing instruments, or songwriting, music therapy can be tailored to meet the unique needs and preferences of each individual.
